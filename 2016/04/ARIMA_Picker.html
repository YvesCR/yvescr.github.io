Shiny gadget to pick ARIMA parameter

Apr 23, 2016

ARIMA is a type of model widely used in time series, for exemple to predict sales or stocks.

The learning curve for this model is steep at the beginning; the time to understand the difference between autocorelation and moving average take a while. Even when you fit your first model, you canâ€™t say that you master all the subtilities of the exercice.

To understand it, it needed me that book that only French reader can understand and that shiny app to finally be confortable to implement arima model in a professionnal environment. That web site, also, is a great ressource.

The arima picker is an add-in made to help to speed the learning curve, mainly by making the simulation and analysis steps faster.

The app is a simplification of the app of @allstatr, to make it fast to run and light to load.

To launch the app as an add-in, you need a recent version of Rstudio(v0.99.878 or later). Otherwise, you still could enjoy the arimaUI function.
